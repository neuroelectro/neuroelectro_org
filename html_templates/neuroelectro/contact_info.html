Contact Information

The NeuroElectro website and framework is developed and maintained by Shreejoy Tripathy (in the lab of Paul Pavlidis at University of British Columbia) and Rick Gerkin (Arizona State University). NeuroElectro was originally developed in the lab of Nathan Urban at Carnegie Mellon University.


The NeuroElectro developers are pleased to receive the expert guidance and direction from Gordon Shepherd (Yale University) and Sean Hill (EPFL and the EU Human Brain Project).
